6 Underground



Synopsis of the Movie

Six people, each the finest in the world at what they do, have been selected not just for their talent but also for a singular ambition, to erase their pasts in order to create the future. They fake their deaths to stay anonymous and leave no footsteps whenever they do their missions. Their missions are just bringing down the bad people from the government. The corrupt, the liers, etc.





Ryan Reynolds - One

He is the mastermind of the whole movie. He was a CEO before he became a ghost which explains his wealth. Since he is very rich, he made a lot of devices to help in their missions. He does not mention his name because he wanted to keep their missions anonymous

Melanie Laurent - Two

She was a CIA Spy before she became a ghost. She is basically the guns of the group. She handles all the shootouts and defends everyone. She is also known as Camille.

Manuel Rulfo - Three

He was a hitman before he became a ghost. Like Two, he is also the guns of the group. The two of them stay side by side protecting the team. He is also known as Javier.

Ben Hardy - Four

He is a traceur, or someone who does parkour. He is essentially the eyes of the group. He scouts out where to go. He is basically a bird. He is also known as Billy.

Adria Arjona - Five

She was a doctor before she became a ghost. As mentioned, she is the doctor of the group and she heals the wounded and fights when needed. She is also known as Amelia.
